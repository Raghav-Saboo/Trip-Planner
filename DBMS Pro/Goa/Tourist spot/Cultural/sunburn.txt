One of Goa’s most popular festivals, The Sunburn Festival is essentially an electronic dance music festival held in Candolim Beach every year in December. A combination of music, food, entertainment and shopping, the Sunburn Festival was ranked as the Ninth Best Festival in the World by CNN in 2009. Apart from that, there are many other interesting activities, such as rock climbing, spa treatments, massages, slippery football and fire juggling, to name a few. Spread over a period of three days, the festival has an array of famous artists who perform at the festival every day. For more information you can visit the Sunburn festival website. http://sunburn.in/.
