Lying on Juhu Tara Road, Juhu Beach is counted amongst Mumbai's star attractions. Groves of palm trees and expansive sandy stretches render immense beauty to the beach. To attract tourists, many amusement parks and food stalls have been set up on the beach. 

This picturesque waterfront is frequented by maximum number of visitors during weekends and holidays. Cycling, jogging and horse and camel riding are some of the activities you can indulge in at the beach.
