Featuring picturesque surroundings that are characterised by endless blue waters of the Arabian Sea and distant Haji Ali Dargah, Worli Sea Face is a posh area in South Mumbai. It is a business district and a major residential area, inhabited mostly by affluent people. Extending from the Worli Fort in the north to Narayan Pujari Nagar in the south, this locality is a popular place of interest in the city. Famous for experiencing giant waves during monsoon season, it attracts locals and tourists alike throughout the year. 

Worli Sea Face features a number of significant attractions that include the Worli Fort, Haji Ali Dargah and Bandra-Worli Sea Link (Rajiv Gandhi Sea Link). Besides, there are a number of high-end restaurants and shopping outlets present.
