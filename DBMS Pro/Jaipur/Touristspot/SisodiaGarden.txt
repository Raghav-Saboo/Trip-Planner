The refreshing greens of Sisodia Rani ka Bagh in Jaipur offers a great opportunity for an idyllic stroll. This garden was built by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh for his second wife who was a Sisodia princess. Sisodia Rani ka Bagh welcomes you with a sense of royalty and grandeur, with murals portraying the love story of Radha-Krishna and many paintings featuring heroes from various battles on display.
